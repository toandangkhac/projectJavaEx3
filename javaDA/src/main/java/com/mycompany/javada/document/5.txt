Legend of Co Loa citadel
When he captured the land of Van Lang, An Duong Vuong changed the name of the country to 
Au Lac, and established the capital in Phong Khe.
The land chosen to build the citadel is a solid hill like rock. Crowds of people are busy 
digging the ground, carrying the soil day after day, the citadel grows taller.
An Duong Vuong and his entourage came to see it with great satisfaction, thinking that a 
citadel could stand forever, thunder and lightning could not shake, rain and storm could 
not destroy it, and enemy troops could not break it.
